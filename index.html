⚖️ SPE Coverage Adjustment — How It Works

A multiplier is applied only to SPE-dependent topics (e.g. Kaizen Events) to account for reduced SPE headcount or late starts. Topics completable by Ops alone or with remote SPE support (e.g. AI, Scorecard, Best Practices, B2B) are not adjusted.

  • ×1.3 — Double coverage (SPE shared from another site)
  • ×1.5 — Late hire (SPE onboarded mid-year)
  • ×2.0 — No dedicated SPE (remote support only)

Example: Site with no dedicated SPE earns 40 pts in Kaizen Events → Adjusted = 40 × 2.0 = 80 pts. Same site earns 80 pts in AI → stays 80 pts (not SPE-dependent).

Seeding & The Draw

Your league performance determines which Pot you land in. The Pots determine your draw. The draw determines who you face. Every league point matters.

🏺 How Pots Are Formed

At the end of Phase 1, each category's sites are ranked by league points. The top 2 from each category go into Pot 1, the next 2 into Pot 2, and everyone else into Pot 3.

🎲 Draw Rules

🛡️

Category Protection

No group may contain two sites from the same category — every group is cross-category by design.

⚖️

Pot Balance

Each group gets one team from Pot 1, one from Pot 2, and (where applicable) one from Pot 3.

🌟

Seed Advantage

Pot 1 teams are placed first and anchored — the two highest overall seeds cannot meet before the Final.

🎲 The Draw Show: The draw will be conducted at the end of May through an interactive animated experience — a digital ceremony that reveals each team's group assignment one by one, building suspense with every click.

💡 How does a "match" work? During a 2-week window, all sites in a group operate normally — pursuing B2B targets, completing Kaizens, driving AI adoption, hitting scorecard metrics. At the end of the window, points are calculated using the World Cup scoring system and sites within the group are ranked. It's a direct comparison of real operational performance measured over the same timeframe.

Maximum Gap: 140 pts

Between a WC Champion (+150) and a group stage exit (+10). Significant but not insurmountable — two strong league months in Phase 3 can close it.

⚠️ No qualification = 0 bonus. Sites below 100 pts in Phase 1 receive 0 bonus points in Phase 3. They continue competing in the league but without the World Cup advantage — making qualification all the more important.

📊 Example: How Bonus Points Reshape the Standings

SITE A
Phase 1: 320 pts
WC Bonus: +10 (group stage exit)
= 330 pts entering Phase 3
SITE B
Phase 1: 280 pts
WC Bonus: +150 (World Cup Champion 🏆)
= 430 pts entering Phase 3

Site B was behind in the league but their World Cup run flipped the standings. Site A now needs to outperform by 100 pts over Sep–Oct to reclaim the lead. Difficult — but absolutely possible.

🎯 By design: The World Cup should matter, but the league must remain competitive until the very last month. That's why the gap is significant but not definitive.
